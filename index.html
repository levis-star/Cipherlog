About Me

The Story

I'm a cybersecurity enthusiast and student with a passion for understanding how systems can be broken - and more importantly, how to secure them. My journey into security started when I was in my diploma educational UDOM, to test small applications if i can login as admin out of curiosity.

Over the past few years, I've been immersed in network security, web application security, cryptographic protocols, malware behavior analysis, and digital forensics. I actively participate in CTF competitions and document my learnings here to give get fedback and important to track my learning works.

This blog is my public notebook — raw learnings, key insights, and real experiences from someone actively learning in theory to practical ways.
